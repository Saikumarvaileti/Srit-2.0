About Us    

Overview of Campus...

SRIT was established by Founder-cum-Secretary Sri Aluru Sambasiva Reddy in November 2007 in memory of his mother, Late Smt. Aluru Narayanamma, to give shape to his firm belief that EDUCATION IS A KEY ENABLER FOR PROGRESS

MOU'S & COLLABORATIONS

The institution has entered into Memoranda of Understanding with many corporate and industrial organisations to strengthen the skill sets it provides its students. Ranging from educational material to soft skills that improve the employability of the students, these MOUs will help SRIT (to) create character-shaping and life changing training modules for students, helping them garner more success in life – as employees and as human beings.

Tata Consultancy Services

TCS IT Employability Program

Created, seeded and nurtured under the CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) segment of TCS, this skill creation and development program covers many different spheres of the students education and personality development.

EduSkills

Eduskills Foundation

SRIT not only offers global quality education, we also try to offer it in modules that make it easier for the students to learn. Towards that end, SRIT has tied up with Eduskills, a foundation that offers digital content and courses of up to five global academy programs, creating LMS (learning management systems) for SRIT’s programs, as and where possible.

UiPath

UiPath

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is one of the most exciting new domains in the world of IT and engineering. SRIT and UiPath have chosen to come together to provide cutting-edge training to the students and faculty of our institution in this technology of the future which will help (to) guide our students towards better placements in the RPA domain.

Cisco Networking Academy

CISCO

CISCO is one of the giants in the digital domain. Who better to deliver and expand the knowhow of our students and the institution by bringing their digital skills into the higher education system? In collaboration with CISCO, we offer world-class technical programs in networking, cybersecurity, cloud computing, automation, RPA and other industry 4.0 skills to our institution (Faculty) and our students.

Microchip Academic Program

Microchip

Allowing our students a variety of programs within various education streams is the aim of SRIT. This enables students to find their own comfortable super-specializations within a given sector. SRIT and the Microchip Academic program offer unique world-class technical programs in networking, cybersecurity, cloud computing, automation, RPA and other industry 4.0 skills to our institution and our students, expanding their digital skills.

Palo Alto Cybersecurity Academy

Paloalto

In a digital world full of actors, cybersecurity of networks and even the cloud is a serious issue, needing specialized training. We have tied up with the Palo Alto Cybersecurity Academy to help our students prepare for cybersecurity careers. They will learn cybersecurity fundamentals, network security, cloud security and how a security operations centre (SOC) works via the hands-on curriculum.

RED HAT Academy

Certificate of MOU

With the Red Hat Academy, SRIT intends to provide students training from the recognized leader of Linux in the IT industry through flexible courses designed for Linux, cloud, and development technologies. It will also provide our students with the foundational skills necessary to embark on other technology topics.

AWS Academy

AWS

Our tie-up with the AWS Academy will help us bridge the gap between industry and academia. As cloud technologies continue to help organizations transform at a rapid pace, employees with the necessary cloud skills are in high demand. AWS Academy provides a ready-to-teach cloud computing curriculum that prepares students to pursue industry-recognized certifications and in-demand cloud jobs.

Blueprism University Academy

Blueprism

This joint effort between SRIT and the Blueprism University Academy will help us deliver customized, role-based, public, and private training to students, academia professors, and more. These courses can be delivered in a classroom setting or virtually, giving students an opportunity to enhance their knowledge, network with the best and brightest, and help them become key players in shaping the future of intelligent automation.

APSSDC

APSSDC is a unique organization formed as a public private partnership (PPP) corporation to promote skill-development & entrepreneurship in the state of Andhra Pradesh. Our MOU with them will allow us to give our students an opportunity to learn the career skills that will help them succeed.APSSDC will also serve the important task of providing high quality skilled manpower as part of the “Knowledge and Skills Mission”.

Virtual Labs

It is a project initiated by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, under the National Mission on Education through Information and Communication Technology. Memorandum of Understanding between Virtual Labs and SRIT for setting-up a Nodal Center of Virtual Labs.

Deltacons Project (India) Pvt Ltd

It has expertise in the field of Geographic Information System (GIS), Digital Image Processing (DIP), Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) Survey. It undertakes aerial and topographical surveys for contour generation, establishing road and transmission line alignment. It also uses Electronic Total Station, DGPS and LiDAR equipment. It provides training to the Civil Engineering students of SRIT in the field of Advanced Surveying.

NHAI

It shall facilitate the faculty, researchers and students of the institution to familiarize with the latest trends in the highway/transportation sector with a common objective of sharing the knowledge in the respective domain. The collaboration of NHAI and SRIT will work on Highway Network Traffic Demand and Incident Management, Highway safety, Highway Work-zone Management, Highway Pavement Management System. Under this MOU, NHAI has also agreed to provide internships for the B. Tech Civil Engineering students of SRIT Anantapur.

AIC-SKU Confederation,Atal Incubation Centre

AIC-SKU Confederation is an Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) at Sri Krishnadevaraya University (SKU) supported by the Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog, Govt. of India and AP Innovation Society, Govt. of Andhra Pradesh. It is a Special Purpose Vehicle registered as a Not-for-Profit Section-8 Company (as per the Govt. of India Companies Act 2013). This collaboration will create and inculcate entrepreneurial spirit in the student community, grooming start-ups ideas and emerging businesses in the sectors which lead to job creation.

Godavari Industrial Products

Godavari Industrial Products Private Limited, is involved in Service activities incidental to oil and gas extraction excluding surveying and other oil and gas field service activities and machinery.

Our joint effort with them will provide our students with real world training through:

  • Setting up lab facilities
  • Training staff with advanced methods of teaching
  • Supporting final year students and R&D engineers in project work
  • Providing technical pieces of training on advanced concepts
